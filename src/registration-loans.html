Simple Fast Loans Registration Loans

Registration loans are a type of personal loan using your vehicle registration as collateral to obtain up to $900* for any cash emergency situation. With registrations loans, there’s no need to own your car outright. Most importantly, you can apply online, get the money you need and make payments from the comfort of your home. Registration loans are only available in Arizona.

What Do I Need to Get a Registration Loan?

With Simple Fast Loans you won’t have to jump through many hoops to get the money you need with your vehicle registration. The following items are required to process your registration loan application:
  • Valid Arizona driver’s license
  • Arizona motor vehicle registration in your name
  • Most recent pay stub
  • You must have an Active Checking account
  • Valid contact phone number
  • Valid social security number
