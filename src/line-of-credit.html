Simple Fast Loans Line of Credit

If you need extra cash for any emergency situation or major purchases, our Line of Credit Loan option maybe right for you. Similar to a credit card or a flex loan, a line of credit is custom made for you by you. Borrow what you need up to your credit limit. It’s not necessary to take the full amount you’re approved for. Simple Fast Loans offers line of credit loans in Alabama ($2,000) and Tennessee ($200 - $1,500).

Benefits of a Line of Credit

  • Draw funds up to your credit limit
  • More flexibility with cash advances
  • Simple, fast application process

How Does a Line of Credit Online Work?

Before you apply online for an easy line of credit loan from Simple Fast Loans you may have questions about the process, because providing you with accurate information is important to us here are answers to the questions we receive the most:

A line of credit gives you access to money any time you need it. You can draw on it up to your maximum limit for a set period of time and only pay interest when you borrow on the line of credit. Once you pay back borrowed funds, that amount is again available for you to borrow. With this flexible borrowing option, you can choose when to take out the money, pay it back and borrow again, as long as you adhere to the terms as well as paying off what you borrow on time and in full.
